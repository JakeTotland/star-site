Members & Officers

New & Prospective Members

Whether you've recently joined Star, or you're still just thinking about it, welcome! Here's a list of suggestions you'll want to consider in order to hit the ground running.

  1. Join our mailing list
  2. Get added to our Slack workspace
  3. Decide if you want to join any of our preexisting project teams
  4. Attend a general meeting
  5. Brainstorm a new project idea
  6. Propose a new officer position you'd excel at
  7. Share Star on social media

Current Members

As a fiercely democratic organization, Star's members are the ones standing proudly at its helm. They meet with their project teams as needed, and attend general meetings when they are able. When we can, we like to recognize the contributions of our members by celebrating their achievements on our Spotlight page.


Are you a current Star member with an awesome new idea? Consider turning it into an official project/position/general proposal! Don't hesitate to reach out to any officer or project team leader for assistance. We'd be more than happy to help where we can.


Official student organizations at Rutgers University are registered on getInvolved, a Rutgers service that keeps track of members, advisers, and other data. This service is accessed with a Rutgers net-ID. For current Star members, it is optional to be on getInvolved. If you wish, you may register yourself here.

Executive Board

These are the dedicated directors of various activities within Star. Executive board members run and are elected once per year, usually during the spring semester. If a Star member believes there is need for a new board position, they can propose their idea to be voted-on at any general meeting. To reveal more details about a particular officer, click their name or picture below.

To get in touch with any of our board members regarding Star, please email ruspacetech@gmail.com and ask to be referred to them.
