Partners

Being a Star partner can mean several different things.

Donors

As a fiercely democratic organization, Star's members stand proudly at its helm. They meet with their project teams as needed, and attend general meetings when they are able. When we can, we like to recognize the contributions of our members by celebrating their achievements on our Spotlight page.

Associates

These are the dedicated directors of various activities within Star's hierarchy. Executive board members run and are elected once per year, usually during the spring semester. If a Star member believes there is need for a new board position, they can propose their idea to be voted-on at any general meeting.
